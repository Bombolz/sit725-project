About me

"Anything that can go wrong will go wrong."

Every time I experience a problem in programming, whether that be syntax, logic error or just seeing someone's else code not functioning as intended, this quote often comes to mind and in some ways I've prescribed to the idea that there will always be errors in programming. However, discovering the problem and resolving it is also one of the great things about programming and the sense of accomplishment by overcoming that problem. With programming, there's always some way to improve on your work, and and I think that's a great thing; to be able to strive towards continuous improvement. I have had diverse programming experiences, from creating a game, to developing applications in VR to coding in both front-end and back-end for web development.

I'm a huge gamer and play all genres of video games, whether that is single-player or multi-player. I have already created a simple space-invaders game during my early years of learning programming, and I hope to continue using my programming knowledge towards developing new and exciting games for everyone to experience.

I'm also a huge fan of music, of all sorts of genres and langauges. Music can communicate emotion through language barriers and I think that's a wonderful thing. I can play the piano and also in my free time I would try and learn about composing music. Music and video games overlap a lot and making a game with good music helps create a magical experience.


Some of my favourite things

One of my favourite games is NieR: Automata; it has daring gameplay elements, a compelling story and emotional soundtracks enough to induce tears all merged together in one package.

One of my favourite romantic-period composers is Chopin. His music is full of emotion expressed throughout in his compositions, one would be his Waltz in C Sharp Minor (Op. 64 No. 2).

I also like cats. I'm not crazy though, I swear.

So now you know more about me! I like video games, music and cats! I would like to think that I'm a friendly and loyal person, albeit sometimes too naive or nice as my friends like to put it. To my future co-workers, I hope to get along with you.
